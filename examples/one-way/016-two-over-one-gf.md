# 2/1 Game Force Continuations (H-22 Drill)

Convention: After a 1-suit opening, a new suit at the 2-level by an unpassed
responder (13+ HCP) is **game-forcing**. Neither player may pass below game.

## Quick Reference

| Opener Rebid | Meaning |
|--------------|---------|
| 2M (own major) | 6+ cards, any strength |
| New suit | 4+ cards, natural |
| 2NT | 12-14 balanced |
| Raise responder | 4+ support |
| 3M (own major) | 6+ cards, extras |
| 3NT | 15-17 balanced |

Responder with 3-card support for opener's major: bid 2/1 first, then
raise to 3M later (forcing -- opener chooses 4M or 3NT).

## Hand 7 -- Cuebid Slam Exploration
