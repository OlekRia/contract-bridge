## Recommendations

### Immediate (Before Next Tournament)

1. **Competitive entry drill (URGENT — 3rd tournament flagging this):**
   - With 20+ HCP combined and a void in opponent's suit: MUST bid.
   - With 6+ card suit and 10+ HCP: MUST compete at 3-level.
   - Review: Board 15 as negative exemplar, T5 boards 6/13.

2. **Strain selection protocol:**
   - Under competitive pressure, prefer the LONGEST suit, not the highest-ranking.
   - With 6 clubs and 4 hearts: bid clubs, not hearts (Board 13).
   - Never play a major suit fit when dummy has 0 HCP (Board 6).

3. **Heart game threshold:**
   - 4H requires: 8+ card fit AND at most 3 top losers.
   - With 4-3 heart fit: stop at 3H or try NT/different suit (Board 14).
   - Check: is HAQ accessible? If both offside, don't bid game.

   - When one hand has 20+ HCP, Blackwood or control-bidding is mandatory.
