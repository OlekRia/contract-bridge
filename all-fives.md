## Defensive Bidding

> **All defensive actions governed by LTC adjusted for vulnerability**

| Vulnerability | Adjustment |
|---|---|
| Favorable (NV vs Vul) | NV vs Vul — act with 1 more loser than standard |
| Equal | Both Vul or both NV — standard thresholds |
| Unfavorable (Vul vs NV) | Vul vs NV — require 1 fewer loser than standard |

### Overcalls

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| Simple | 8 losers at equal (9 fav, 7 unfav), 5+ cards, suit quality > HCP |
| Jump | Weak, 9 losers at equal (10 fav, 8 unfav) |
| Takeout X | All unbid suits, 6 losers at equal (12+ HCP or any 17+) |

## Defensive Carding

> **Use your head first. Signals are a last resort.**

### Attitude

| Signal | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Low | encourage |
| High | discourage |

**Exception:** Ace lead → give count (even/odd)

### Count

| Signal | Meaning |
|---|---|
| High | even |
| Low | odd |

### Italian Style (on discards)

| Discard | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Odd card | Interest in discarded suit |
| Low even | Interest in lower suit |
| High even | Interest in higher suit |

### Opening Leads

| From | Lead |
|---|---|
| Length | 3rd or 5th highest |
| Sequences | Top of sequence (AKx→K, KQx→K, QJx→Q, JTx→J, T9x→T) |
| AK | K lead from AK; A asks for count |
| xx | Higher |
| xxx | MUD (middle-up-down) |
| xxxx | Highest safe, then low |
| With J | Lead 4th highest (KJ732→3) |

**Vs suits:** Prefer sequences; avoid unsupported honours
**Vs NT:** Longest suit unless bidding says opponents hold it
